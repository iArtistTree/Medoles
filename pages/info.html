    • Medoles (/mɛˈdoʊlz/ MEH-DOHLS), officially the Most Serene Republic of Medoles, is a North American micronation and enclave within Canada. Located in the archipelago of the Thousand Islands with a land area of just over 25 hectares and a population of 3 as of 2024.

    Medoles takes its name from Medoles Point, one of the few named geographical features of the area. The name could have derived from the word medole, defining as the soft and juicy essence of a plant. It relates to the soft nature of the soil at Medoles Point.
