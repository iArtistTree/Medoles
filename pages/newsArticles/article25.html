Senate Race Heats Up With 6 Candidates

Author(s): TheArtistTree
3rd of July 2025

As we hit July, we are also hitting our 12th Senatorial election. Due to time constraints, we'll keep this article shorter than usual. There are six candidates in this election: Moss, Tom Ombre, Sherlock Chow, Xesphor, Quinnlyn, and Mymy.

1. Moss
The first is Moss, also known as "nachosunrise", they are a new citizen wishing to make their mark on the political sphere as an independent candidate. They will be launching their career by stating:

"I am great full to announce my canidancy for the wonderful and blessed senate, I have a large bill it’s called THE OVERHAUL BILL and it’s gonna be amazing, we are gonna make this nation greater than it ever was, this nation was always good but it can be better it can be GREAT. Let it be known that I may not pump out a bill every other day but when I make a bill it will be for the people and to help everyone, they will be BIG and EXTREMELY GOOD BILLS!

VOTE MOSS FOR SENATE!"

2. Tom Ombre
The second on the list is Tom Ombre, the founding member of the recently established National Prosperity Party of Medoles (NPPM). When asked about his campaign, he says:

"My fellow Citizens, I will not try to win you over with noxiously honied or vitriolic words and rhetoric but simply with a list of bills in the planning stages and the making.

- Policing Reform Act | Final Revision
- Bureau of Archivists Bill | Final Revision
- Civil & Criminal Reform Bill | Draft
- Provincial Expansion & Protection Act | Revision
- Governmental Transparency Act | Draft
- Outreach & Aid Bill | Draft
- Inactivity Clause | Revision

Make your choice wisely.

Tom"

3. Sherlock Chow
Our third candidate, SherlockChow is a senior member of Medoles representing the Medoles Popular Front (MPF). He hopes to be elected back into the Senate stating:

":3

Hello fellow citizens of Medoles! Saluton kaj salut!

My name is Sherlock, although you may know me in the server by "Mx. Chow", and I come to you to ask for your vote in the upcoming senatorial elections.

I believe in democracy, autonomy, freedom and the wellbeing of every medolite sibling, I've been a member of the Medolite Popular Front (the oldest still-standing party of our nation, which works to teach and uphold the values of Marxism) and senator of our nation in several instances. You could say I have a bit of experience legislating.

My plans are simple: keep a rigid stance on democracy and all of its principles (Free and frequent elections, elected public officials, freedom of expression, association and of the press); make our government more transparent; make way for a better system for citizens to propose legislation; secure the laicity and equality within our constitution if the need for a reform arises and finally, work with the executive and legislative sectors to create events and make our community more welcoming to all people."

4. Xesphor
Xesphor is the fourth candidate to have signed up. He is the current sitting senator representing the Innovation Party of Medoles (IPM. He will attempt to defend his seat in the Senate by saying:

"I would like to say that for my second term my goals are bigger than my first term.
So far what I've done is make provinces of medoles official and the process is yet to be finished.
Going forward i want to make Medoles more inviting by giving provinces more of a purpose and give more than just simple colourful election maps."

5. Quinnlyn
The fifth candidate in this race is Quinnlyn, also known as "tired_pride". They will be representing the Party of Gay Depression (PGD) which thrives for a better future. About her campaign, she said: "A representative of the citizens of Medoles, I have only your best interest in mind. And the safety of the citizens, which includes keeping all rules and regulations in place."

6. Mymy
Mymy is the sixth and final candidate for Senator. They are familiar faces in recent elections and strive to make their mark as an independent candidate. In their campaign speech, they stated: "dawg please js vote on me"
