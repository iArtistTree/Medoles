The two candidates for Senator: Birdish, and Blappa

Senator Elections: Fresh Start

Author(s): TheArtistTree
7th of May 2025

We are in May, and it's exam season. This means some tough times are ahead for some folks, but fear not! As Summer creeps in, so are new Senatorial elections! With busy times, I'll have to keep the article short for this one time. This election will have two brand-new independent candidates: Birdish and Blappa.

The first candidate is Birdish, who was a former citizen of Medoles in January and has recently come back to run as a candidate for Senator. When asked about his campaign, he replied: "If you vote for me, I will bring judicial reviews to Medoles."

The second candidate is Blappa. While relatively unknown in Medoles, they have participated in other democracy-oriented groups. They commented: "Vote me because i am a cat."

This will be a unique election, as no parties are participating and there are no recurring candidates from past elections. This could be a breath of fresh air for the Senate.
