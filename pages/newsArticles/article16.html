First Bill Ever Rejected, Nation Divided

Author(s): TheArtistTree
8th of March 2025

The first legislation to be declined by the Parliament occurred earlier this week. Indeed, Senator CherlockChow forwarded a bill to the Parliament seeking to establish relations with the Kingdom of Salanda. President TheArtistTree suggested the proposal after being reached out by the Salandarian government.

The bill was deemed controversial after it drew a lot of attention regarding the stance of the monarch and head of state of Salanda for the Israel-Palestine conflict. While some were adamant about establishing said relations, the poll ended with 2 in agreement, 3 in abstainance and an overwhelming 6 outright shutting down the bill.

This makes it the first bill officially shut down by the Medolite government. Could this potentially lead to a divide in the government with differing opinions? Stay tuned for more as we are drawing closer to the arrival of a new Senatorial election.
