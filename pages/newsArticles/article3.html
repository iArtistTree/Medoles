Elections Take an Unexpected Turn

Author(s): TheArtistTree
20th of June 2024

After Medoles had reached five citizens, the inaugural elections were on their way until they met an unexpected end. On the 11th of June, elections kicked off with the signups and received two plausible candidates. It means there would be only two feasible outcomes from these elections: X-Man and Sabermoose.

From what should have been a tight race with both candidates having points to prove and fight for, one of the candidates, X-man, had to pull out minutes before the voting ballot opened. When asked to elaborate on why he dropped from the race, X-Man responded: "No comment." It means that Sabermoose is, without a doubt, declared the 1st President of Medoles.

Will X-Man make a return for the following elections? And how will Sabermoose set a precedent for future presidents to abide by? Only the future can tell. Until then, we'll have to wait as the next elections on the list for the position of Mayor will open up on the 30th of June 2024.
