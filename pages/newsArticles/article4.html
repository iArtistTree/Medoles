Parliament Passes Its First Historic Bill

Author(s): TheArtistTree
22nd of December 2024

After a return to normalcy this holiday season and the re-election of X-Man, the parliament has finally opened its doors. Indeed, the Mayor Tree has begun the push for bills, with the first one about opening relations with another Canadian micronation, Westnia (formerly "Westania").

After 3 days, the result has been overwhelmingly positive to initiate our foreign policies. The Medolite citizens made it clear as the inaugural bill ended with a unanimous vote of 5-0 for establishing relations with Westania. It is the first of many bills on their way in the coming months for change.

The elections for Mayor will begin on Sunday the 29th of December 2024.
