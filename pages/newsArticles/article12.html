The results of the first Senatorial elections

Medoles Welcomes Its First Senators

Author(s): TheArtistTree
3rd of February 2025

With the new governmental positions in place and the inaugural Senate election coming to an end, our first two senators are revealed. Indeed, the results for the two first people filling up the seats of the Senate of Medoles were officialized last week, Friday afternoon.

After the votes were locked in, here are the results: PinkMappre took the first seat with a 3-vote cushion and grabbed this first seat ahead of SherlockChow. In the second round, SherlockChow fell short again behind Helium by a singular vote. With that in mind, PinkMappre and Helium will be the first members of the Senate of Medoles. We'll see what the Senate has to offer for the Medolite population.

On another note, the Presidential elections are underway as we speak.
