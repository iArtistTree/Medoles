Candidates for Presidential Elections are Set!

Author(s): TheArtistTree
18th of June 2024

The nation has slowly been growing in the past weeks, and with that in mind, the elections have finally kicked off after a long wait. The signups were open on Sunday & Monday, and we're here to reveal the candidates participating in the first presidential elections of Medoles.

The first candidate to sign up is X-man, one of the founding members of the Medoles. The independent candidate had this to say to launch his campaign: Hello, I am X-Man and I am running for the 1st presidential election of Medoles I plan to setup diplomacy and the staff team for Medoles so that the nation can startup with quick growth and stable staff.

X-man won't be alone in this election. On the other side of the ring, we also have Sabermoose, the first non-founding citizen to have joined Medoles earlier this month. He'll be campaigning under the banner of the Conservative Party of Medoles and opens with the following speech: The goal of my Presidency would be to draft a longer and stronger constitution and establish diplomatic relations with other micronations

These elections ought to be an engaging presidential campaign to see who will earn the title of the first president. This is without mentioning more positions will also be decided on that are also up for grabs including the mayor and judge in the following weeks.

The vote will be open on Thursday the 20th of June 2024.
