Medoles Chess Club on the Rise

Author(s): TheArtistTree
20th of February 2025

The Medoles Chess Club (MCC), the main chess governing body in the micronation, recently joined a chess league. The MCC is known for the AI Chatbot Championship started last month, a competition between AI Chatbots currently on hiatus. They've recently taken another step by joining a competitive league for all Medoles to participate.

Last week, the MCC applied to join the Bundesliga. The Bundesliga is a blitz chess tournament on the website of Lichess where chess teams battle it out. The league has multiple divisions of varying team strength, featuring a promotion and relegation system where the top teams move to a higher division; the opposite is true at the bottom of the leaderboard.

At the time of publication, the Medoles Chess Club played on the 16th and 20th of February, picking up two successive promotions to go from the 18th division to the 16th. They will play to battle for a 3rd successive promotion this week on the 23rd of February at 2 PM EST.
