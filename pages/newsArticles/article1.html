A New Beginning

Author(s): TheArtistTree
6th of June 2024

The micronation of Medoles is opening its doors, inviting you to join its vibrant community. Located in the United Counties of Leeds and Grenville, a micronation named Medoles declared its independence on the 24th of January 2024. The newly formed Medolite government has meticulously planned the future of the micronation, focusing on its democratic regime and how its people will forge their own path in this next chapter.

With this in mind, the emergent micronation has taken the next step towards a better tomorrow. The Medolite government now allows people to sign up and go through the process to obtain citizenship. Citizens of Medoles are not only free to interact with its community, but they can also contribute and partake in its politics, shaping the future of Medoles. Citizenship will be available to be givern out on their Discord server.

The first set of elections for Medoles will be coming in soon, keep a close eye on them.
