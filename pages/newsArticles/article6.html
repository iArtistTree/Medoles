Medoles Reveals Their New Coat of Arms

Author(s): TheArtistTree
28th of December 2024

As matters begin to settle, Medoles still needs to define certain characteristics that determine the Medolite identity or even functional ones like the currency, capital or even an anthem. Those questions looming among the people are finally starting to get some answers as new legislation passed yesterday night.

Bill #5: "Bill to Adopt a Coat of Arms" has passed with an astonishing 6-0 and a 75% turnout, the highest turnout as of the 27th of December 2024. The bill proposes the use of a coat of arms as a stand-in symbol for the nation.

The coat of arms was designed by TheArtistTree, and it features a purple shield with a white, gold and red tricolor band running along the right side of the shield. The purple has a distinct pattern of triangles and lines that characterize the landscape and nature of Medoles. Finally, the national flower, the Herb-Robert, is featured prominently in the center.
