Two Bills Passed: Government's Agenda Gains Momentum

Author(s): TheArtistTree
25th of December 2024

With the parliament opening its doors last week, it has made significant progress in advancing legislation, setting new precedents in Medoles. After approving the first bill to establish relations with Westnia (formerly Westania), Mayor Tree moved forward with the government's agenda, proposing two more bills, with the results revealed earlier today.

The first bill suggested expanding their plans to grow their foreign influence by creating a list of relations where nations can be classified relative to their relationship with Medoles. The second bill further emphasizes it by forwarding a bill to join the GMNC (Great Micronation Census), also known as the GRMN (Grand Recensement des Micronations) in French. It's a French and English-speaking organization where micronations can meet and share announcements, descriptions and relations.

Both bills passed with relative ease and no opposition on the 24th of December 2024, the governmental agenda is going according to plan and begins to gain momentum. X-Man, the President of Medoles, expanded on this when he exclaimed: "For the second term of my presidency, I am working towards expanding Medoles' diplomatic influence within micronation communities and setting a foundation for further diplomacy within Medoles."
